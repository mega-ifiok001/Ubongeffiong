Solution: Implemented role mapping, decision matrices (RACI-style), escalation protocols, and KPI-integrated dashboards to distribute authority and standardize workflows.
Outcome: Reduced founder involvement in routine decisions, improved accountability & reporting clarity, created investor-ready operational structure for sustainable scale.
